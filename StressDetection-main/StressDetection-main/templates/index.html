About

Our app helps you recognize and manage stress by detecting emotions from your text, voice, and video inputs. Using advanced AI technology, we analyze your expressions and tone to provide real-time insights about your emotional state.

Why Stress Detection Matters

Stress affects millions of people worldwide and can lead to serious health problems if left unchecked. Early awareness and management are key to maintaining mental well-being. Our app empowers you with the tools to understand your emotions and take proactive steps to reduce stress.

Help & Support

How to Use the App

1. Detect Emotions via Text: Go to the "Detect Emotion" section, select "Text Emotion," type your message, and click "Detect Emotion" to see the result.
2. Detect Emotions via Voice: Choose "Voice Emotion," then either upload an audio file or use your microphone for live detection.
3. Detect Emotions via Video: Select "Video Emotion" to upload a video or record one using your camera for emotion analysis.
